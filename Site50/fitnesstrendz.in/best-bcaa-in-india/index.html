Side Effects of BCAAs

The use of BCAA supplements is generally safe and without side effects for most people But moderation is the key. If your intakes between 1535 grams per day so there will be no side effects.

One thing you should know is that if you are suffering from Amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS) so don’t use BCAAs. In this disease, your nerve cells break down and the main symptom is muscle weakness.

If you use BCAAs in such conditions so it might give you a negative impact on your health. I suggest you discuss this with your health expert before adding this to your diet.
