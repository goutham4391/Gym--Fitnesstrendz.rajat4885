Multivitamin is a basic essential nutrient which our body needs but if we use this in excess quantity so yes there will be some consequences you may face.

As we know, Multivitamin contains minerals such as calcium, iron, magnesium, potassium, and zinc and our body needs these minerals just to fulfill our daily vitamin requirements.

If we take large doses of these minerals for a longer period so it may cause side effects such as stomach bleeding, muscle weakness, tooth staining, increased urination, uneven heart rate and confusion.

But as I said large doses. If you consume maximum 3 multivitamin capsules in a day so there will be no such side effects will occur and you will get all the benefits which required.
