What is pre workout

Pre workout supplement is basically an energy supplement that gives you an extreme push before exercise. One thing you should know about pre workout, it is not your whey protein or BCAA which you can take throughout your life.

You should use pre workout only when you feel tired or exhausted. Never make a habit of taking pre workout on a daily basis. If you are using 30 serving pack so use it wisely when you feel you need it, and extend it for at least 2 months.

This supplement is only for those who have the age of 18 and above. If you have a blood pressure problem so don’t use any pre-workout supplement. Every pre workout contains caffeine which is not ideal for BP patients. Also, never take pre-workout before sleep. There should be at least a 5-6 hour gap between your pre workout intake and your sleep.

It will also prevent muscular fatigue that allows you to perform more reps before your muscles give out. It will Improve body composition that leads to greater increases in lean muscle mass.

Improve Endurance: It has citrulline which improves your endurance performance. Increase mental focus: As well as physical effects, pre-workout enhances mental focus alertness, reaction time, perception of energy, and positive vibes. If you’re a regular exerciser, you’ll know that half of the success comes from mentality — if your head is in the right place, your body will follow. FAQs

Q1. What does pre-workout do?
Pre-workout is a dietary supplement used by athletes and weightlifters to enhance athletic performance. It is taken to increase endurance, energy, and focus throughout a workout.

The pre-workout supplement includes various potent ingredients such as caffeine and creatine to enhance performance and help to achieve endurance before attempting the hardcore workout.

Q2. How long does a pre-workout last?
The maximum duration of pre workout lasting will depend upon many factors, such as age, weight and muscle mass, the great and sort of the complement you are taking, standard diet, including the quantity of water you get daily.

In general pre workout takes 20-30 minutes to align with your body and the maximum benefits you will get for 1 hour max but as you know that it contains caffeine so the effect of caffeine may last upto 4-5 hours.

Q3. When to take pre-workout?
I have already inform you this , An individual must maintain a time of 20-30 minutes consuming the pre-workout supplements before starting your exercise.

For those who are taking it in the evening so make sure there should be at least a 4-5 hours gap between your sleep and pre workout consumption. Be cautious about the timings you intake your pre-workout. If you are making plans on taking it some hours before getting some sleep, then it should be avoided.

Q4. How many carbs in pre-workout?
The recommended amount of carbohydrates and protein to take in earlier than your strength workout is usually 30-40 grams of both carbs and protein, that too before 60 -90 minutes before your exercise. But these days most pre-exercise supplements are carb and calorie-free.
