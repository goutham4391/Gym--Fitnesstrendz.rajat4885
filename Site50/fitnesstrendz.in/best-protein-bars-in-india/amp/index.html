What is Protein Bar

Protein bars basically healthy snack bars which is a big part of the fitness world. It is a great way to supplement your diet, whether you can use it as a snack alternative or use it as a post-workout. Protein bar specially designed to be delicious and packed with healthy ingredients.

Protein bars are usually made of soy, dates and dried fruit, nuts and seeds, and whole grains like oats or quinoa. Apart from this, It has both whey and casein protein. One protein bar can provide you around 10-20 grams of protein and 5-10 grams of fibre depending upon the brand.

Protein is one of the most essential macro-nutrients for the human body. It can be found in a variety of foods including meat, fish, eggs, chicken, and some plant-based sources like soy, nuts, beans and pulses, etc. But when you are looking for an on-the-go snack to curb your hunger between the meal, then nothing can be better than protein bars.

It is a great alternative to chocolates which is high in calories and fat. One protein bar a day can help you curb your appetite and support muscle recovery after workout. Now, let’s take a look at the benefits of having protein bar.

Benefits of Protein Bars

1. Continuous Energy Supply

Protein bars are fully loaded with protein, fibre and dietary nutrients that gives you continuous energy. it is highly preferred and you can use it during hardcore workouts, traveling, and running. They can fill you with energy on a busy, dull, and strenuous day.

2. Ideal for Weight Loss

Protein bars keep you full for longer hours that keeps you away from high-fat junk food. It also boosts metabolism and can help to maintain your muscle and strength. But you need to choose a protein bar that is low in calories and sugar otherwise it won’t help you much in your weight-loss program.

3. Great Mid Meal Snacks

One of the biggest reasons of weight gain is mid-meal munching. Protein bars are a tasty and healthy alternative for you to kill those hunger pangs before the next meal. Unlike the usual snacks like chips and chocolates, protein bars don’t cause any blood sugar spikes that are one of the primary reasons for weight gain.

4. Easy Availability

One of the best reasons of protein bars is that you can carry them on the go and they last long in any weather, unlike shakes, eggs or meat. On top of that, you need to prepare other dishes that need time and resources but on the other hand, Protein bars are ready to eat and don’t need to be refrigerated.

5. Best Pre-Workout & Post Workout

Athletes and fitness fanatics need much more protein and energy than other people. Protein bars are a great way for them to get that much-needed boost of energy right before a workout or after one. Apart from this, it also helps to recover the after workout muscle soreness and build muscle tissues.

6. Perfect Meal Replacement

So many protein bars are available in the market that contains a high amount of protein and fibre along with several essential minerals and vitamins so that you get well-balanced nutrition. It can be used as a substitute for a meal when you’re in a hurry or just not in the mood for a full meal. But Don’t make a habit of it as it only works occasionally.

7. They’re delicious!

Protein bars are made with all the tasty ingredients that make them delicious in taste. It is kind of boring to eat the same meal every day. especially when you are on a strict diet plan, so you can add a protein bar as an alternative to your meal once in a while to keep the continuity of your diet plan.

8. A complete healthy product

Protein bars contain omega-3s, magnesium, and are rich in vitamins. They also contain healthy fats that promote heart health, balance hormones, control sugar levels, and maintain your appetite. It has a lot of antioxidants, that help in weight management and reduces inflammation.

9. Helps in Weight gain

As you know that to gain weight you need to consume more calories than you burn in a day. There are so many protein bars available in the market that provide around 350-400 calories and an easy way to add calories without having to eat a lot of extra food.

10. Muscle gain

As per the research, an active person or a fitness fanatics require minimum 1.5 gram of protein per kg of body weight. Protein bars can be a convenient way to add protein and minerals to your diet and helps you achieve your muscle-building goal.

FAQs

Are protein bars healthy?
Yes it is, but when you consider purchasing a protein bar, always check their nutrition value such as (Amount of protein, sugar, fat, fibre, etc.) 

How much serving of protein bar per day is safe?
You can use 2 protein bars in a day but consider it as a snack and not a proper meal because it won’t give you all the micronutrients you get from a proper meal.
