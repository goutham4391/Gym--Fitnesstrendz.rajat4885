Food to gain weight

I have already shared weight gainers detail information and how they work but I never force to buy one. There are so many reasons which may not allow you to go with mass or weight gainer. It can be your family who stops you from buying it or maybe your pocket doesn’t allow you.

But don’t worry, there are plenty of foods which you can add to your diet can make your weight gain efforts both healthy and more effective.

Here are 7 foods to help you gain weight or add muscle without spending money on mass gainers.

1. Milk

Milk has been considered as a weight gainer or muscle builder for decades.

It is a good source of calcium as well as other vitamins and minerals and has a balanced protein, carbs and fats.

It provides you with the perfect combination of casein and whey proteins that lead to greater mass gain than other protein sources.

2. Red Meats

We all know that non-vegetarian foods are really high in protein but red meats are one of the best muscle-building foods available.

It provides you around 3 gram of leucine per 200 gram of serving which is the main amino acid your body needs to stimulate muscle protein synthesis.

Also, it increases muscle-building hormone IGF-1 which helps to gain weight.

3. Starchy carb foods

If you are a student or facing the budget issue and don’t able to spend too much on your diet so these low-cost foods are perfect for you.

These foods are very high in carbs and calories to help you gain weight which includes, Potatoes, Oats, Corn, Beans and legumes and Quinoa.

They basically increase your muscle glycogen which leads fuel source for most sports and activities.

4. Whole Eggs

Eggs are considered as healthiest muscle building food on the planet. It contains high-quality proteins and healthy fats.

As long as your goal is to gain weight, I recommend you to consume a whole egg. The yolk contains in it gives you healthy carb which energizes you all day long.

If you are non-vegetarian and not allergic to eggs, there is no need to limit your egg consumption. You can easily have 3 whole eggs per day without any issue.

5. Full-Fat Yogurt

Full-Fat Yogurt has an amazing nutrition profile which includes perfectly balanced protein, carbs and fat.

As you eat more during your weight gain period so it also helps you to improve digestion and food absorption.

The best part about this food is that you can include so many things like fruit, Chocolate into it and make it delicious snacks.

6. Dried Fruit

All the dried fruits are very beneficial in weight gains because they are a high-calorie snack that also provides antioxidants and micronutrients.

They are very convenient to eat and taste great. You don’t need to waste time to cook anything and you can carry them anywhere.

Also, you can add these dry fruits in so many Indian dishes and make them more delicious and healthy.

7. Dark Chocolate

A bar of high-quality dark chocolate has so many health benefits. It provides you tones of antioxidants and helps you lower your stress.

It is very high in calorie which helps you to increase your weight but one thing you must know, always go for dark chocolate with a cocoa content of at least 70%.

100 gram of dark chocolate provides you almost 600 calories which are exceptionally high and along with that, you get all the fibre, magnesium and antioxidants available in it.
