How is whey protein made?

Basically, whey protein is made from milk and there are two primary parts of milk, casein and whey. These are major sources of whey protein As of now, the protein available in the market is generally extracted from cow milk. However, there are some other animals being used for whey production which includes, buffalo, goat and camel etc.

Earlier, whey protein powder is a by-product of the cheese-making industry. But when they realise that the economic markets of whey are way more profitable than cheese so they start manufacturing whey as their primary product to maintain its quality.

Basically, there are 3 types of whey protein available in the market, whey concentrate, whey isolate and whey hydrolysate. whey hydrolysate is very expensive so most people used the rest two whey proteins.
