How to take Creatine

There is two-phase to take creatine. The first is loading and second is no loading. In the loading phase, you need to take 20 grams per day for initial 6 days and then maintain 3-5 grams per day.

When to take Creatine

If you are choosing Loading phase option so the best way to take creatine is to 3-4 divided doses during the day and if you are using no loading phase so you just need to take 3-5 grams post-workout in your whey protein shake for better result.
