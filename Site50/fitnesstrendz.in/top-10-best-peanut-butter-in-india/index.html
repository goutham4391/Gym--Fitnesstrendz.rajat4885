Peanut Butter Types

All peanut butter is good in taste and we will not discuss the flavour, that you can pick according to your taste but there are some different types of peanut butter you should know.

1. Crunchy

As per some health experts, crunchy peanut butter is slightly healthier than its creamy counterpart but there is no difference between their nutritional value. Both types of peanut butter generally have an equal amount of calories, fat, sugar, and carbs but crunchy peanut butter contain less saturated fat and has 2% more fibre per serving. If you are a fitness enthusiast so this is the best type of peanut butter for you.

2. Creamy

As per National Peanut Board, 56% of people prefer creamy peanut butter. The reason behind this is that women and children enjoy creamy peanut butter more, while men lean toward crunchy peanut butter. It totally depends on you whether you want to go for creamy or crunchy, both are equally good in terms of taste and nutrition.

Homemade Peanut Butter Recipe

There is no rocket science behind peanut butter production. All you need are some basic ingredients and you can make it at home without spending any extra bucks on the packaging or delivering etc.

So I will share with you the simple procedure of making peanut butter step by step and you have no idea how amazing and easy it is to make. so let’s get started with the recipe.

Ingredients:

2 cup Peanuts
1/3 teaspoon salt
1-2 tablespoons honey

1. Roast the peanut in a non-stick pan at the low-medium flame for about 10 minutes until it gets lightly golden and glossy with oil.
2. Add the peanut to a kitchen towel and just simply rub them with your fingers to remove their skin and then sieve the peanut in a pot.


3. Now, add the shelled peanuts into a grinder and add 1/3 teaspoon salt in it and then pulse the grinder a couple of times (depending on how smooth or crunchy peanut butter you want). Open the grinder lid and mix it properly with the help of a spoon and again pulse it on low speed for about 30 seconds until the oil has started realising out.
4. Open the grinder lid again, add 1-2 tablespoons of honey into it and mix it well. Now, pulse it again for another 30-45 seconds.
5. Now add the mixture to a storage container. Refrigerate it and turn it upside down in the fridge so the peanut oils and solids can re-mix and there your easy and tasty peanut butter is ready to eat.

FAQs

Q. What is the difference between peanut butter and butter?
Peanut butter is a paste made from dry and roasted peanuts. It is a highly-digestible, healthy, tasty treat packed with powerful nutrients and healthy fats. whereas butter is made from milk. It is good in taste but not that health-friendly.

Q. Can it be used with chapattis for weight loss?
Yes,peanutbutteris suitable as a spread on chapatti. It is rich in protein, and promotes satiety and weight loss. You can use it with bread or milk smoothie etc.

Q. What is the suggested dosage of peanut butter?
The suggested dosage is 2 tablespoons (32g) per day if you are a beginner and 4-5 tablespoons (130g) per day if you are a fitness athlete.
