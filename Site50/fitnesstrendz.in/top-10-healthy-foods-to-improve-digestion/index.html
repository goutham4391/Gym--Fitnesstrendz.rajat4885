1. Curd

Yoghurt or curd is a great Probiotic (an ingredient that contains live bacteria). These good and beneficial bacteria are known to improve gut activity, soothe inflamed digestive systems and treat an upset stomach.

Yoghurt is an excellent source of good bacteria. It is good for your teeth and bones. but If you are eating curd during the day, have it without sugar but if you are eating curd at night, add sugar or some black pepper. It will assist in digestion and calm your digestive system.

Curd has a moisturizing effect on your skin and it heals your dry skin naturally and it will also help to reduce high blood pressure, As per the United States Department of Agriculture, a cup of yogurt (250grams) contains about 275mg of calcium which is very helpful to maintaining bone density.

2. Spinach

Spinach is loaded with tons of nutrients in a low-calorie package. Dark, leafy greens like spinach are important for skin, hair, and bone health. They also provide protein, iron, vitamins, and minerals.

Spinach Leafy greens have a powerful antioxidant called glutathione, which can help keep your liver working right. you can use spinach in your daily salad and have it raw for better results.

Spinach is also high in fibre and water, both of which help to prevent and promote a healthy digestive tract. Spinach contains large quantities of Vitamin A which is necessary for the growth of all bodily tissues, including skin and hair.

3. Papaya

Papaya is one of the best food available in India. It contains carotenoids that can neutralize free radicals and reduce oxidative stress in older adults and people with prediabetes.

Papaya is very beneficial for digestion also it contains pantothenic acid, copper, fibre, folate, Vitamin A and magnesium it also has an enzyme called papain which helps break down protein fibres and assist in the digestive process.

It will also help you in bloating problems as well, and if you are having constipation problems and want to get rid of this so add papaya in your daily diet.

4. Chia Seeds

Chia seeds are a good source of fibre, providing 11 grams of fibre in 28-gram of serving. It also high in Omega 3 fatty acid which is very important in order to improve your bad digestion.

Chia seeds are a rich source of high-quality fibre and very low in calories that makes you full for a longer period. In other words, it can beneficial for your weight loss.

The fibre in chia seeds is mainly soluble fibre and mucilage, the substance responsible for the gluey texture of moistened chia seeds. these fibres also help to lower cholesterol and slow down digestion, which can prevent blood sugar spikes after eating a meal and promote a feeling of fullness.

5. Fish Oil

Fish oil can be obtained by eating fish or taking supplements. Fish that are especially rich in the beneficial oils known as omega-3 fatty acids. Fish oil is most often used in supplements for conditions related to the heart and blood system.

Research suggests that consuming fish oil during pregnancy may help reduce the risk of Asthma in children and it will also beneficial to reducing inflame allergy during pregnancy.

Fish oil is also used for many kidney-related problems. it’s kind of hard to eat fish on a daily basis so instead of eating, you can take 1-2 fish oil capsules which is equally beneficial in terms of digestion.

6. Banana

As we know that banana is a rich source of potassium and Vitamin B6. A medium-sized banana can provide about a quarter of your daily vitamin B6 which helps you from non-alcoholic fatty liver disease.

A banana will also help to weight loss, yes we all know that banana is high in carbs but the other properties of bananas are very important for weight loss they are rich in Electrolytes also it’s improve digestion and very important for your bones and brain.

It also contains pectin fibre, potassium, and magnesium which increase your bone strength and make them stronger. It is high in carbs that make you fill-up for a longer period.

7. Ginger

Ginger is used globally as a spice and flavouring agent. It is most often enjoyed in its fresh, dried, and powdered forms. especially in India almost everyone using ginger in early morning tea.

Ginger is a portion of amazing food and It is a good source of vitamins A, B, C, and E as well as minerals such as calcium, iron, and magnesium which is very effective for fatty liver and improve digestion.

Ginger can help to prevent gas and bloating by relaxing the muscles in the gastrointestinal tract. This can help soothe an upset stomach. Ginger can also help decrease belching and flatulence and can help lower the pressure put on the opening between the oesophagus and stomach.

8. Beetroot

We all know that fibre is the main source of healthy digestion and Beetroot is a good source of fibre. 1 cup of beetroots contains 3.4 grams of fibre and 4 gram of protein which helps you in weight management as well.

Beetroot contains a pigment called betalains that reduce kidney inflammation. it will also reduce the risk of chronic diseases including colon cancer, heart disease and type 2 diabetes.

It will also prevent digestive conditions like constipation, Ulcerative Colitis, Gallstones and Irritable Bowel Syndrome. Beets are low in calorie and high in water which helps you to achieve and maintaining a healthy weight. Also, it is one of the best ways to increase haemoglobin levels.

9. Kiwi

Kiwi is again great fruit to improve digestion. It has plenty of fibre which helps you to maintain healthy digestion. Also, It has a proteolytic enzyme called actinidin which breaks down the protein and enhances the digestion.

Kiwi has also boosted the immune system. it is a great source of Vitamin C which is an essential nutrient when it comes to boosting your immunity. It will also protect you from cold and fever.

It also contains potassium that treats both constipation and loose stools. Kiwifruits contain a peptide called kissper that is known to fight inflammation in the colonic tissues.

10. Cantaloupe

Cantaloupe is one of the best fruits in both taste and nutrition value. It is almost 90% of water which makes you hydrate all day long and helps in weight management and healthy digestion.

It is also rich in fibre which prevents constipation and makes you full all day long. along with that, it will also reduce the risk of heart disease and diabetes.
